Why PUNCHED! ?

PUNCHED! stands out for its commitment to pushing boundaries and setting new standards in the fitness industry. Our smart boxing gloves are equipped with state-of-the-art sensors and connectivity features, allowing you to track your performance, analyze your technique, and monitor your progress like never before. We understand that every punch counts, and that's why our gloves are designed to be your ultimate training companion.

Our Company

At PUNCHED!, we believe in the power of innovation to transform ordinary workouts into extraordinary experiences. Our journey began with a vision to create a product that seamlessly merges technology with the art of boxing, providing enthusiasts with a smarter, more engaging training routine. With our roots deeply embedded in the dynamic tech scene, we set out to craft boxing gloves that would not only protect but also empower.

The PUNCHED! Experiance

Innovative Technology: Our gloves are packed with sensors that capture and analyze your every move, providing real-time feedback to enhance your technique.
Connected Fitness: Stay connected to your progress through our user-friendly app. Track your punches, calories burned, and even challenge friends to virtual sparring sessions.
Quality Craftsmanship: Designed in Israel, our gloves are not just technologically advanced but also crafted with precision and durability, ensuring a reliable partner for your fitness journey.
Passionate Community: Join the PUNCHED! community of fitness enthusiasts, share your achievements, and be inspired by others on the same journey.
